Getting Started

  • Press and hold the main switch for 6-8 seconds to activate Access Point (AP) mode.
  • Connect to WiFi network "StairLight_AP" (password: 12345678).
  • Navigate to 192.168.4.1 in your browser to access this page.
  • Use the LED1 and LED2 tabs to adjust settings.
